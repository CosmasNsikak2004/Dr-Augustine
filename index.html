About Me

AUGUSTINE MICHAEL IMIKAN Ph.D B.Sc (Unical), PGDE (Uniport), PGDEE (Unical), M.SC (ESUT), Ph.D (Uniuyo) Department of Geography and Natural Resources Management Faculty of Social Sciences, University of Uyo, Uyo.

Dr. Augustine Micheal Imikan is a distinguished scholar with a Ph.D. in Geography and Natural Resources Management from the Faculty of Social Sciences. With a passion for understanding the complexities of our planet's landscapes and ecosystems, Dr. Imikan has dedicated his academic journey to unraveling the intricate relationships between human societies and the environment.


Throughout his career, Dr. Imikan has conducted groundbreaking research, contributing significantly to the field of Geography and Natural Resources Management. His work has focused on complexities of our planet's landscapes and ecosystems, and his innovative findings have been published in reputable journals and presented at international conferences.
